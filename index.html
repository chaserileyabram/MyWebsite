Chase Abram

I am a PhD Student in the Kenneth C. Griffin Department of Economics at the University of Chicago. My research is broadly concerned with understanding the mechanisms generating inequality, and how policy can address related issues. Currently, I am developing tools for modeling dynamics in quantitative spatial models, with an eye towards better understanding gentrifying forces in urban settings. Additionally, I am working on improving our understanding of dynamics in economies with production networks and adjustment frictions.
